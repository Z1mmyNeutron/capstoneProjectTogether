Our Project is a create a website tailored for students dubbed "The Study Room"

The website has several subsections: A Pomodoro Alarm setup, A goal tracker, and an ambient music player

The Pomodoro section is for students who want to use the Pomodoro studying technique to maximize information retention and productivity

The goal tracker lets students visualize their progress by writing what tasks they need done and what's been accomplished

In the ambient music player students can choose from a list of "moods" they want to foster throughout their study session

If the Pomodoro timer is active the music player will play music during study time and will pause during breaks.
